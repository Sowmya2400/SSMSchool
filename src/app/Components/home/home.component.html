Welcome to SSM!

Where Excellence meets with Experience

SSM, in its 36 glorious years of existence, has grown to provide a learning environment that facilitates the broadening of the intellect and grooming of the personality. The school was inaugurated on 11 June 1986. It is a co-educational school, with classes from preschool to 12th, and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. Montessori system of education is followed for the children in the age group of 2 ½ to 7 ½ years (Pre-KG to class II level). The children then continue in the regular stream of education.

“A good teacher can awaken joy in their students and leave a positive impression that lasts a lifetime.” In Sunshine SSM we have highly qualified, trained and experienced faculties who are capable in bringing out the uniqueness in each child.

The school is managed by SSM Educational Trust, a public charitable non-profitable trust, established in 1990 with an object of establishing, maintaining, managing and developing educational institutions for providing quality education to the students. SSM is motivated by passion to be a leader in education.

Facilities

LUNCH

In order to inculcate healthy eating habits and to develop social skills during mealtime we, at SSM provide nutritious meal for our students.

TRIPS AND TOURS

SSM arranges an Educational Tour (from classes 6th to 12th) and Field Trips (from classes 3rd to 12th) to explore the beautiful world in an educational way.

LABORATORY

The Science and Maths Labs available in SSM are of International Standard with trained Lab Assisstants which teaches students in the most practical way.

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Students are provided with Swimming, Sports and Fine Arts Classes, which includes Arts & Craft, Karate, Photography, Violin & many more..
